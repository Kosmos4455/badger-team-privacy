PRIVACY POLICY FOR BADGER TEAM EXTENSION

Last updated: January 6, 2025

1. Introduction

BADGER TEAM ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles information when you use our services on olympdate.com. We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of your information and are committed to processing it responsibly and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

2. Information Collection

Our extension collects and processes the following limited data solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Chat Messages: Access to messages within the olympdate.com platform for automated response functionality. This is necessary for the extension to perform its primary function of message automation.
  • User Interface Interactions: Basic interaction data required for the extension to function properly, including click events and navigation between chats.
  • Website Content: Limited access to specific webpage elements on olympdate.com that are necessary for the extension's operation.

3. Use of Information

All collected information is:

  • Processed locally within your browser
  • Used exclusively for providing the extension's core functionality
  • Never transmitted to third parties
  • Not stored permanently or shared externally
  • Not used for any purpose other than the extension's primary functions

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information:

  • All data processing occurs locally within your browser
  • No personal information is stored on external servers
  • The extension operates solely within the specified domain (olympdate.com)
  • All communication with our API server is encrypted and secure
  • Data is automatically cleared when you close the browser or disable the extension

5. Your Rights and Control

You maintain full control over your data through the following means:

  • You can disable the extension at any time through Chrome's extension manager
  • No personal data is retained after disabling the extension
  • You can remove the extension completely at any time
  • You have the right to know what data is being processed
  • You can control the extension's functionality through its interface
